Thanks for participating! Here's how to use this extension.

Once the questionnaire starts, it will start recording both your screen and your voice.

When something great happens, click the button.

When something bad or confusing happens, click the button.

The Next button will take you to the next question. Please note, once you advance, you can't go back.

Tips:

  • Remember to speak your mind – Talk your way through what you're thinking in each step.
  • Your screen is being recorded – Go ahead and DO the activity rather than just talking about it.
